## Conventions

- **Green bond** (inter-layer physical leg): dim = 2
- **Black bond** (virtual bond χ): dim ∈ {8, 16, 32}
- **Cavity** (`gbp_cavity.png`): n×n×2 lattice; x− face open (red triangles = external legs on T/B, not tensors); centre inter-layer bond intact (green, dim 2); other three faces one merged boundary tensor each
- **Neighborhood** (`gbp_neighborhood.png`): n×n×2 lattice; four grouped face messages; center inter-layer bond → open external legs on T/B (▲/▼, not tensors)
- **sc** = log₂(largest intermediate tensor elements)
- **tc** = log₂(total contraction FLOPs)
- Optimizer: TreeSA (`ntrials=20`, `niters=60`, `βs=1:1:18`)
